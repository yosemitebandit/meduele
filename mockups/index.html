How does Me Duele work?


1. Patients call
650-123-1234



Patients call our hotline, select their language, and then leave a message detailing their symptoms.

2. Volunteers select cases.



Volunteers select these messages, review notes from previous calls, and then call the patient.

3. Volunteers provide advice to patients.



Volunteers speak with patients about their symptoms and provide medical advice
